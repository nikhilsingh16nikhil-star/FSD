The State Board of Technical Education (SBTE), Patna, Bihar, is responsible for evaluation and certification of six-semester Diploma Courses of all the Polytechnic Institutions affiliated to the SBTE Bihar. It has been constituted vide govt. order no. 75/Dir dated 31st May, 1955 under the Science, Technology and Technical Education Department, Government of Bihar.
